About Zaatar

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Zaatar was created with a vision to bring the rich heritage of authentic Arabian and Middle Eastern cuisine to Bengaluru. What started as a passionate culinary dream quickly became one of the city's most loved destinations for premium dining, family gatherings and authentic Arabian flavours.

The restaurant was founded by a team of food enthusiasts and hospitality professionals who wanted to recreate the true taste of traditional Arabian recipes while combining them with luxurious interiors, modern presentation and world-class customer service.

Inspired by the vibrant food streets of the Middle East, Zaatar focuses on serving freshly grilled BBQ, authentic mandi, shawarmas, seafood delicacies, tandoori specials and handcrafted signature platters prepared using premium ingredients and traditional spice blends.

From juicy charcoal grilled Alfaham to sizzling Arabian platters, every dish at Zaatar is carefully crafted by expert chefs with years of experience in Middle Eastern cuisine.

Today, Zaatar has become a popular dining destination across Bengaluru, known for its luxurious ambience, premium hospitality and unforgettable food experience. With multiple branches across the city, the restaurant continues to serve thousands of happy customers every week.
